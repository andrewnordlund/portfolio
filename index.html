About Me

I'm a programmer with an emphasis on web accessibility. By day, I have over ten years experience in auditing websites against WCAG, and now I'm a COBOL programmer on a large financial application. By night, I have a plethora of side projects and hobby sites/apps.

Despite my emphasis on accessibility, I am a developer, not a designer. I can make it work. I probably can't make it pretty.

This site is a place where I can link to everything all in one place, or at least describe it.
